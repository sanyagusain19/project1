💜 WHY THIS PROJECT?

More than a project, it became a complete learning experience.

Network Builder AI was created after realizing that students often struggle to find the right people, projects and learning resources. Instead of searching endlessly across different platforms, this application intelligently recommends developers, GitHub repositories and YouTube tutorials based on shared interests.
