THE STORY

Every project begins with a problem.

💭 The Beginning

I wasn't looking for another project.

I was looking for a solution.

During my learning journey I realized that finding people with similar interests was much harder than learning programming itself.

College gives us knowledge, but outside the classroom developers spend hours searching for GitHub repositories, YouTube tutorials and communities that align with their goals.

I wanted a platform that could recommend all of these in one place instead of requiring endless searching across different websites.

That simple frustration became the foundation of Network Builder AI.

😕

The Problem

Developers often struggle to discover people with similar interests, quality GitHub repositories, and reliable learning resources. Finding everything manually takes time and usually breaks the learning flow.

→
✨

The Solution

Network Builder AI combines developer matching, GitHub recommendations and YouTube learning resources into one intelligent platform, making learning collaborative and efficient.
